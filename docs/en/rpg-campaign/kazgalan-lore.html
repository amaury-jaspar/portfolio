Kazgalan - Lore

This is the short version of the lore for my RPG campaign : Kazgalan. The game was originaly in french and I decided to keep all the original names to preserve the original feel (also I like the idea of non french speakers struggling to pronounce these words {include appropriate baguette or surrender jokes} ). Feel free to use all the content here as you please.

Geography, Demography et Topography

The adventure takes place in Kazgalan, a country situated in the land of Aminexil. The country covers an area of a bout 300 000 km² (about the same size as today's Poland). 15 milion people currently share this beautiful and prosper land.

Kazgalan is surrounded by 3 other countries : Teratai, Vermello and Colgagor (For more details, please see the map below)

Some history

The country just recently got out of an 8 years long war (1559-1567) with Colgagor, a nearby country. Both countries decided they had already suffered too many casualties and agreed to cease the conflict. After the war, the former gouvernment was overthrown and replaced by a democratic republic regime. Kertrand Kravache, a hero of the war against Colgador was elected as president with an overwhelming majority (96 % of votes). The former officer played a major part in the resolution of the conflict with the ennemy. He managed to engage a discussion between the two countries and eventually convinced them to make peace.

The current story takes place in 1577, 10 years after the conflict. We are now in a reconstruction era. Life is prosper, employement rate is through the roof and the population seems enthusiastic about the new life they're building. But the recent events left a heavy mark in people's minds.

Kertrand Kravache, the current president is extremely popular, he was involved in many universally acclaimed reforms such as : free education, lowering the taxes, opening the borders... He is on his second mandate and hopes to be reelected in the upcoming elections, later this year. His re-election is very likely, considering that his approval rating still fluctuates around 85%. Furthermore, there are very few opponants. Politics is not the population's main concern at this time. They are happy with the current system and do not see any reason to change it (If it ain't broke don't fix it). Nevertheless, some aspects of the current presidency are criticized. One of the first act of Kertrand Kravache as a president was to rename every city in the country so that they would begin with a K. A very odd and controversial law, that raised some questions regarding the mental state of KK.
